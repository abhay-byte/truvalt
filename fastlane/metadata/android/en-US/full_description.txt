<b>Your passwords, your rules — zero-knowledge encryption, self-hosted or offline.</b>

truvalt is the password manager that puts you in complete control of your digital security. With military-grade encryption and a choice between cloud sync or fully local offline storage, your credentials are exactly where you want them — and nowhere else.

<b>Key Features:</b>
<ul>
<li>Secure password vault with AES-256-GCM encryption</li>
<li>Passphrase & passkey storage</li>
<li>TOTP 2FA code storage with live countdown</li>
<li>Security & recovery code storage</li>
<li>Secure notes for sensitive information</li>
<li>Password generator and passphrase generator</li>
<li>Vault health check with Have I Been Pwned breach monitoring</li>
<li>Import from Bitwarden, LastPass, Chrome, and more</li>
<li>Encrypted export in .truvalt format</li>
<li>Biometric unlock (fingerprint/face)</li>
<li>Cloud sync with self-hosted server OR fully local offline mode</li>
</ul>

<b>How It Works:</b>
truvalt uses zero-knowledge encryption, meaning your master password never leaves your device. All your data is encrypted locally before it's ever transmitted or stored. The server only sees encrypted blobs — it cannot read your passwords even if it wanted to. You hold the keys to your data.
