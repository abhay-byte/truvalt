⚠️ Account Deletion

Delete Your Truvalt Account

This page explains how to permanently delete your Truvalt account and all associated data. This action is irreversible.

⛔
This cannot be undone

Deleting your account permanently erases all vault items, credentials, and cloud data. There is no recovery option. Make sure to export any data you want to keep before proceeding.

How to delete your account
  1. 1
    Open Truvalt on your device

    Make sure you are signed in with the account you want to delete.

  2. 2
    Go to Settings

    Tap the Settings icon in the bottom navigation bar of the app.

    Bottom Nav → Settings
  3. 3
    Scroll to Danger Zone

    Scroll to the bottom of the Settings page to find the Danger Zone section.

    Settings → Danger Zone
  4. 4
    Tap "Delete Account"

    Tap the Delete Account option. This button is only visible when you are signed in to a cloud account (not in offline/local-only mode).

  5. 5
    Read the warning and confirm

    A warning dialog will list everything that will be deleted. Tap Continue to proceed to the confirmation step.

  6. 6
    Type DELETE to confirm

    In the confirmation dialog, type the word DELETE (all caps) in the text field, then tap Delete My Account. Your account and all data will be permanently erased.

What gets deleted & what is retained
🔐
All vault items Deleted

Passwords, login credentials, credit cards, secure notes, and any other items stored in your vault.

📁
Folders & tags Deleted

All organizational structures including folders and tags associated with your account.

☁️
Cloud profile & credentials Deleted

Your Firebase Authentication account, stored email, and all Firestore profile data.

📱
Local device data Deleted

All locally encrypted vault data, auth tokens, and preferences stored on your device.

📋
Aggregate analytics May be retained 90 days

Anonymized, non-identifiable analytics or logs may be retained up to 90 days for security auditing purposes, after which they are automatically purged.

Alternatives to full deletion
🔒

Lock Vault

Temporarily lock your vault without losing any data. You can unlock it again any time with your master password.

📴

Switch to Offline Mode

Enable Local-only Mode in Settings to stop cloud sync. Your data stays on-device without touching the cloud.

📤

Export First

Before deleting, use Settings → Export to save your vault to a JSON or CSV file for use in other password managers.

Need help or want manual deletion?

If you're unable to access the app, or want to request deletion of specific data without deleting your full account, contact us and we'll process your request within 30 days.
